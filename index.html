Creating an automated smart touch panel for home involves integrating various technologies and components to control and monitor different aspects of your home, such as lighting, temperature, security, and entertainment systems

Here's an overview of how it works:

Components Needed:

  1. Touch Panel:
    You'll need a touchscreen display or a tablet that serves as the interface for controlling your smart home. This can be wall-mounted or portable.

  2. Microcontroller/Controller:
    A microcontroller (like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or specialized home automation controllers) acts as the brain of your system. It communicates with various devices and sensors and handles user inputs from the touch panel.

  3. Smart Home Devices:
    You'll need various smart devices compatible with your chosen controller. These can include smart lights, thermostats, door locks, cameras, and more. Ensure they use communication protocols like Wi-Fi, Zigbee, Z-Wave, or Bluetooth.
